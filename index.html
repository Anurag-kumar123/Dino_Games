Dino fun game

The simple dino game, but with a twist.



This game uses face-api model with pre-trained images to detect faces and their expressions, and makes our lovely dino jump.

    Just keep following things in mind while playing :

  • Make sure your face is well lit.
  • Don't wear any glasses.
  • Don't chuckle right before breaking the hi-score.
